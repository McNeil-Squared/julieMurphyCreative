About

Since 1993, I've been providing branding and identity services for aggressive startups and established companies on the move and other well known larger companies. But this experience goes far beyond your typical logo, business card, letterhead, stationery design packages. Yes, I handle a lot of these bread and butter branding needs, but most businesses don't follow such a predictable pattern. Or maybe you need a trade show booth and vehicle wrap designed, and the deadline was yesterday. Maybe it's a package design for a hot new product. It could even be designing a set of collectable trading cards. With a background in traditional illustration, photography, web design and graphic design for print, I have the breadth and depth of experience to deliver an elegant solution on time and on spec.

Old school meets the digital long before computers became a viable tool for visual and graphical communications, I was hard at work mastering the basics. Fundamentals such as anatomy, life drawing,perspective, fontography, color theory, and layout were stressed along with developing a proficiency with such tools of the trade as ink, pen, pencil, paint and brush. Along the way I also developed an interest and expertise in photography, product package design and web design
